You are an expert at consolidating rule lists. You receive a list of atomic rules extracted from an AI system prompt. Many are near-duplicates or sub-variants of the same instruction.

## Your task:
Merge, deduplicate, and consolidate the rules down to exactly {target} rules.

## Merge strategy:
- Combine rules that express the same instruction in different words
- Merge sub-variants into a single, broader rule (e.g., "use formal French" + "use vouvoiement" → one rule)
- Keep the most specific, testable formulation when merging
- Preserve rule_code numbering as R01..R{target}
- Keep source_section from the most representative original rule
- Keep is_explicit=true if ANY merged rule was explicit

## Output format:
Return a JSON object: {{"rules": [...]}} with exactly {target} rule objects, each having: rule_code, text, source_section, is_explicit.
